Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-24

MailXpert (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides an email classification tool that connects to your Google account to help you organize messages. This policy explains what data we access, how we use it, and your choices.

What data we access

  • Google account basic profile: your email address and display name (scopes: openid, userinfo.email, userinfo.profile)
  • Gmail data (scope: https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.readonly): message metadata and content necessary to classify emails

How we use your data

  • Authenticate you via Google OAuth
  • Fetch recent Gmail messages in read-only mode to compute categories using our AI model
  • Display classification results back to you in the app

Data storage and retention

  • Access tokens are stored only for your active session to serve your requests.
  • We store derived results (classification labels and message IDs) to avoid reprocessing. We do not sell or share your data.
  • You can clear stored results from the app at any time, and you may revoke our access in your Google Account settings.

Your choices

  • Revoke access: visit Google Account > Security > Third‑party access
  • Request deletion: email us at taminderjeet123@gmail.com

Security

We use industry-standard security practices and request the minimum scopes necessary. We never request Gmail send/modify permissions.

Contact

For any privacy questions, contact taminderjeet123@gmail.com.
